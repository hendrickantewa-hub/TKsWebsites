Meet the Dentists

Expert Care, Personal Touch

Our dental team brings years of clinical excellence and a genuine passion for patient wellbeing.

Dr G.T. Pack
B.Ch.D · Principal Dental Surgeon

Dr G.T. Pack is the founder and principal surgeon of the practice, with over two decades of dedicated service to dental health in Windhoek. A graduate with a Bachelor of Dental Surgery, he established his Central Windhoek practice with a clear mission: to deliver world-class dental care to every Namibian. Renowned for his calm chair-side manner and meticulous clinical technique, Dr Pack puts patients at ease from the very first consultation. Under his leadership the practice expanded to Windhoek North Medical Centre, bringing quality dentistry closer to more families. He remains a committed advocate for community oral health education across Namibia.

🦵 Principal Surgeon
Dr Hendrick
B.Ch.D · Associate Dentist

Dr Hendrick joined Pack Dentistry bringing fresh expertise and a specialisation in cosmetic and restorative dentistry. Holding a Bachelor of Dental Surgery with post-graduate training in aesthetic dentistry and digital smile design, he is highly skilled in veneers, composite bonding and orthodontic treatment. Dr Hendrick has a natural rapport with younger patients and families, and is passionate about combining cutting-edge technique with the warm, unhurried approach the practice has always been known for. Patients frequently commend his thoroughness and his ability to explain procedures in plain, reassuring language.

✨ Cosmetic & Restorative
